Austin Language Center Istanbul

Just a minute on foot from Cevahir Alışveriş ve Eğlence Merkezi - Cevahir shopping center in the lively modern centre of Istanbul, this English language school is housed in Golden Plaza with 5 contemporary classrooms. Students will benefit from a computer lab, free Wi-Fi and small class sizes where teachers encourage conversation. There’s also a cafeteria where you can hang out with your fellow students over a rich cup of Turkish coffee or tea. With the school’s proximity to Cevahir shopping center , you can shop till you drop or try some of the city’s best Turkish restaurants after class. It’s also a major public transport hub, so you’ll have all of Istanbul at your fingertips!

Courses & prices

Choose your course. Each of our courses has a special focus, allowing you to direct your studies in the way that best suits you.

1-row. General courses

It’s a fact: there’s no more efficient way to learn a language than using it and being exposed to it in different contexts every day over a length of time. This is precisely what our general courses offer you – the opportunity to receive outstanding language lessons as well as endless opportunities to put your new skills into practice. And that’s how you cement knowledge!

2-row. Private language courses

For some people, a language course in a group may not be the best option for learning a language. We have a solution that offers personalised tuition in a unique setting: immersion language courses in a one-on-one environment. This course type matches learners with an experienced language teacher in the country of their choice. The result? A personalised learning experience that delivers the fastest possible language progress.
