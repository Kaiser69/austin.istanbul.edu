Why study Austin?

We believe in the power of language to change lives and the world. This strives us to constantly improve and develop new methodologies to make the process of language learning worthwhile.

01.

Our Mission

Austin Language Centre (ALC) offers classes that are diverse in nature with its students coming from all around the world. Studying in ALC, exposes students to various cultures, traditions and making friends for life, besides improving their communication skills .

02.

Our Vision

To take the lead in teaching English Language globally for foreign and local students in a fun and creative approach.

03.

Our Approach

To offer superior education in the English Language for international and local students, we emphasize on teaching General English and preparing them successfully for the IELTS examination as entry to college, university and job positions.
